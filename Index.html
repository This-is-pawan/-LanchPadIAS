about Launch IAS

At Launch IAS, we understand the challenges that come with Preparation of UPSC and State PCS Examinations

With experienced faculty, rigorous training, and personalised guidance, we offer a focused and result driven approach to UPSC Exam Preparation. With the help of technology, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional learning methods and the evolving needs of UPSC aspirants. Our institute is dedicated to guide aspirants to achieve their dreams of serving the nation and making a meaningful impact on society. Join us to embark on a journey towards achieving your dreams and securing a bright future in the realm of civil services.
